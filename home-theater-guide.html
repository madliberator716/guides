Setup Guide

Samsung S90D · Google TV Streamer · Samsung HW-Q900F
Google TV Chain Only — see Stremio guide for Shield/lossless audio notes

Audio Priority — Google TV Chain (Best → Worst)
⭐
DD+ Atmos (EAC3 Atmos) — Best for this chain
⭐
DTS 5.1 — Good; passes through eARC as-is
✔️
DD+ 5.1 (EAC3)
✔️
DD 5.1 (AC3)
❌
TrueHD Atmos — Google TV cannot bitstream; TV decodes to PCM
❌
DTS-HD MA — Samsung TV strips to core DTS over eARC
❌
PCM 2.0 — Avoid
NoteFor TrueHD or DTS-HD MA passthrough, use the Shield TV Pro connected directly to the soundbar HDMI IN, or Xbox Series X instead.
Best File Types — Google TV Chain
✅
4K WEB-DL x265 10bit + DD+ Atmos
✅
1080p x265 + DD+ / DTS 5.1
❌
Avoid for this chain: TrueHD Atmos, DTS-HD MA, LPCM

If You See "Dolby Multichannel PCM"
Causes
·
Paramount+ bug on Google TV
·
Google TV not bitstreaming
·
TV not in Pass-Through mode
·
File uses TrueHD Atmos (Google TV cannot bitstream)
Fixes
✅
Use Samsung TV app for Paramount+
✅
Restart Google TV
✅
Check Surround Manual settings
✅
Switch to DD+ Atmos files for this chain
✅
For TrueHD files → use Shield TV Pro via soundbar HDMI IN
